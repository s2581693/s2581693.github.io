Aloha Quick Service

Aloha QuickService POS offers an easy-to-use, advanced point-of-sale solution that reduces training time and increases restaurant efficiency while helping you maximize the life and value of your investment.

Your customers expect quick, convenient service and a consistent experience while your business requires mission critical technology. Aloha POS provides them all.


Key features and benefits include:

  • Easy-to-use, intuitive software – reduce training time
  • Touch screen interface pre-configured graphics – deliver fast, accurate service
  • Integrated electronic payment processing – provide payment alternatives
  • Automatic order routing to kitchen – increase speed and order accuracy
  • Built-in redundancy – avoid data loss and POS system downtime
  • Shift settlement and entry of cash deposits at POS – reduce time in back office
  • Centralized POS management – quickly update pricing, menus and promotions
  • Modular, flexible design – implement a solution that grows with your business

In the hospitality industry, Aloha POS means quick service — and that means stronger sales.
